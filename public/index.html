Creativity |ˌkrēāˈtivədē|

.01

Aesthetic Education

We provide a musically and artistically abundant, architecturally rich environment for the children because we believe sensibilities are the source of all creativity.

.02

Reggio Emilia Inspired

The Reggio Emilia-inspired approach to learning is a project-based curriculum that is flexible and emerges from children’s ideas, thoughts, questions, and observations.

.03

Japanese Education

Japanese early kids education is an inclusive approach which focues on cultivating children's rhythm of life and a sense of thankfulness. Children's will are highly respected.
