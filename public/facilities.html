The Environment

The environment of the school is viewed as the "third teacher". It should be a reflection of the children, teachers, and parents who live and learn there. The environment should be thoughtful, imaginative, attractive, and respect the image of the child.

The Surroundings

Located at the outskirts of downtown Taipei City, the school sits at the root of Yangming Mountain, right next to Zhīshān Yán and Wài Shuāng Xī. The richness of the natural surroundings opens up various possibilities for outdoor activities and nature education.
